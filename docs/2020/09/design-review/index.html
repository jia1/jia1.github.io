Design Review

11 Sep 2020 @ 6.41 pm
#software #documentation #design

In my team at Indeed, I was tasked with the challenge of driving a design review. Indeed has an open culture regarding design reviews. Anyone can join and tune in to any design review session. And anyone can ask questions about the solution. As a start, Indeed has a template for the design review document. I copied the template and filled in the blanks. It felt a little too similar to filling up a Google form (which isn’t great).

It took me an afternoon to fill in the blanks and my team’s feedback was that there are still some missing information which we should be conveying to the design review audience. I took in the feedback, added what’s probably missing (just the bullet points) and hosted a meeting with my fellow engineers to ensure I’m on the right track.

Yes, I was. After dealing with my other task, I returned to the design review document and made the necessary changes. I am still WIP, but here’s how our design review template looks like now:
